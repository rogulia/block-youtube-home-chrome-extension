# Privacy Policy — YouTube Home Blocker

_Last updated: 2026-06-06_

**YouTube Home Blocker — Redirect & Focus** is a browser extension that
redirects the YouTube homepage to a URL you choose. This policy explains exactly
what the extension does and does not do with your data.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any
personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, no remote server, and no user
account.

## What is stored, and where

The only piece of data the extension stores is the **redirect URL** you set in
the popup. It is saved using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API, which
keeps it inside your own browser profile (and, if you are signed into Chrome,
syncs it across your own devices through your Google account).

This value never leaves your browser and is never sent to the developer or any
third party.

## Permissions

- **storage** — used solely to save and read your chosen redirect URL.
- **Host access to `www.youtube.com`** — required so the extension can detect the
  YouTube homepage and redirect it. The extension does not read, modify, or
  transmit the content of any page.
