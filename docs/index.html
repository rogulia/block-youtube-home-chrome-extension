Questions

Good to know.

Does this block all of YouTube?

No. It only redirects the homepage feed at youtube.com/. Search, individual video pages, subscriptions, history and channels all keep working exactly as normal.

Where does it send me instead?

Any URL you choose — a task list, calendar, dashboard, or a blank page. The default is your Todoist Today view, and you can change it in one click from the toolbar popup.

Will it slow YouTube down?

No. It's a few kilobytes of plain JavaScript with no background process. The redirect runs before the page renders, so you never even see the feed load.

Does it collect or send my data?

Never. There's no tracking, no analytics and no accounts. Your redirect URL is stored only in Chrome's sync storage and never leaves your browser. The full source is on GitHub.

What if I want the feed back temporarily?

Open the popup and flip the on/off switch. Redirecting pauses until you turn it back on — no uninstalling required.
